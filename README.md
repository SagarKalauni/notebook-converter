# Notebook Converter

**Designed for Lindenwood University Data Science Students (DSCI 356000)**  

The **Notebook Converter** is a web-based tool created to help Lindenwood University Data Science students easily convert and submit their Jupyter notebooks as **PDF files**. This ensures that students can share their work in a clean, professional format without worrying about formatting issues or compatibility.  

## Features  

- Upload your Jupyter notebook (`.ipynb`) and instantly convert it to a **print-ready PDF**.  
- Maintains code, outputs, and markdown formatting for clear presentation.  
- Simple, user-friendly interface for fast and hassle-free conversions.  

## How to Use  

1. Go to [Notebook Converter](https://sagarkalauni.github.io/notebook-converter/).  
2. Upload your `.ipynb` file.  
3. Click **Convert** to generate a PDF version of your notebook.  
4. Download the PDF and submit it as required for your coursework.  

## Purpose  

This tool is specifically designed to streamline the submission process for Lindenwood University Data Science students, allowing them to focus on learning and coding while ensuring their work is submitted in a professional, standardized format.  

## Notes  

- Ensure your notebook is saved and runs correctly before conversion.  
- All outputs, plots, and markdown content will be preserved in the PDF.
